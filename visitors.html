Overview:

IIIT Bhagalpur is one of the leading IIIT amongst 25 institutes established by MHRD, Government of India. The institute has been granted the status of Institute of National Importance as per the act of parliament. It started functioning under the mentorship of IIT Guwahati from the year 2017. Currently we have students from three branches namely CSE, ECE & Mechatronics Engineering.

The total strength of the 2021-22 batch is 100 and department wise breakup is as follows:

CSE: 49 students

ECE: 39 students

Mechatronics: 12 Students

Additionally, we are having the 8th semester as a free semester for industrial internship. In that case our students can join from Jan 2022.

With the rigorous education and training, constant exposure through invited talks of experts and leading practitioners, technical fest and other various academic activities, our students would be able to contribute their best to the leading organizations and prove invaluable human capital for them.

Our curriculum and laboratories have been developed under the guidance of leading experts in the respective field in the alignment of latest trends and challenges of IT, automation and allied industries. Our students are well versed and possess the requisite and sufficient skill-set in the various domain areas relevant to the industry and corporations such as C, C++, Data Structures, Algorithms, Java, Python, Frontend (HTML, CSS, JS), Backend (SQL, PHP, DJANGO, FLASK), Android Development, Data Science (AI/ML, Deep Learning), Data Analytics, MATLAB, TCAD, VLSI Design Tools, Ansys, Verilog, Robotics and others.
